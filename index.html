👥 Team Roles — 5 People, One Goal: Maximize Final Cash
Each person owns their domain. Paste data every ~5 minutes and everyone acts on their role's command.
👑Person 1COMMANDER
Role
Commander — Final Decision Authority
📋Reads the Command Box and announces the current priority action to the team
✅Approves ALL purchases (machines, kits) before anyone clicks anything
📣Calls out contract upgrades/downgrades — says "switch now" or "hold"
💰Tracks cash balance and approves spending vs. the $1M+ target
🔚Manages end-game wind-down: sells machines Day 180+, cuts kits Day 190+
⚡ Continuous — every data paste
🏭Person 2STATION MONITOR
Role
Station Monitor — Bottleneck Hunter
📊Watches St1, St2, St3 utilization — enters them into the ⭐ Live Metrics panel
🚨If any station hits ≥92% util, immediately tells Commander: "Station X is red, buy now"
⚠️If ≥85% and trending up over 2 data pulls, warns Commander to prepare purchase
➕On Commander approval: clicks the + button to buy a machine on the correct station
💸Day 180+: Sells machines on any station below 55% util (click − button)
👁 Every data pull — compare to last pull
📄Person 3CONTRACT MANAGER
Role
Contract Manager — Revenue Optimizer
📈Monitors Avg Lead Time — enters it into the ⭐ panel and watches revenue pills
🚀LT < 0.5 days and on C1 or C2: tell Commander "ready for Contract 3 upgrade"
⬆️LT < 1.0 day and on C1: tell Commander "ready for Contract 2 upgrade"
🔴LT exceeds contract limit: immediately alert Commander "downgrade NOW, losing money"
🔬Also manages Station 2 priority: switch to "Pri Step 4" when St2 queue is building
👁 Every data pull — LT is the key metric
📦Person 4INVENTORY MANAGER
Role
Inventory Manager — Kit Supply Chain
📦Monitors "kits 1" queue — enters Kit Queue into ⭐ panel
🚨If Kit Queue > 3 jobs: immediately alert Commander "kits starving — raise ROP now"
⚙️On Commander approval: updates ROP or Quantity in the Material panel (ONE change only)
🔚Day 190+: gradually reduce Order Qty each session to avoid holding dead stock at Day 268
💡Rule of thumb: ROP = 2× daily demand. Qty = enough to last until next order arrives (4 days)
📦 Watch kits queue every pull

⏱ Every 5 minutes — set a timer
